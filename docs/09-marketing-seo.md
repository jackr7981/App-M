## Marketing Strategy

### Value Proposition

**Primary Message**: "Find All Maritime Jobs in One Place"

**Secondary Messages**:
- "AI-Powered Job Aggregation for Bangladeshi Seafarers"
- "Your Complete Maritime Career Platform"
- "Never Miss a Job Opportunity Again"

### Unique Selling Points (USPs)

1. **Automated Aggregation**: Jobs from 10+ Telegram groups in one app
2. **AI-Powered Parsing**: Standardized 8-field format for every job
3. **Free Forever**: No subscription fees, always free for seafarers
4. **Mobile-First**: PWA works offline, no app store required
5. **Bangladesh-Focused**: Built specifically for Bangladeshi mariners
6. **Trusted Agencies**: Only MLA-verified manning agents

---

### Brand Positioning

**Brand Voice**: Professional, Helpful, Trustworthy
**Tone**: Conversational yet authoritative
**Personality**: Tech-savvy friend who helps you succeed

## Target Audience

### Primary Segments

#### Segment 1: Active Job Seekers (60% of users)

**Demographics**:
- Age: 25-40
- Experience: 2-10 years at sea
- Current Status: Between contracts or unhappy with current ship
- Income: $4,000-$8,000/month
- Location: Dhaka, Chittagong, Khulna

**Psychographics**:
- Goals: Find better-paying job, advance career
- Pain Points: Missing job opportunities, fragmented information
- Behavior: Checks job groups 3-5 times/day
- Tech: Uses WhatsApp, Telegram, Facebook daily

**Message**: "Stop checking 10 groups. See all jobs in one app."

---

#### Segment 2: Maritime Students (25% of users)

**Demographics**:
- Age: 20-24
- Status: Maritime academy students (BMMTA, MAMI, etc.)
- Current: Final year or recently graduated
- Income: None (seeking first job)
- Location: Near maritime academies

**Psychographics**:
- Goals: Land first job, connect with alumni
- Pain Points: No experience, no network
- Behavior: Highly engaged on social media
- Tech: Digital natives, smartphone-first

**Message**: "Start your maritime career with BD Mariner Hub"

---

#### Segment 3: Onboard Seafarers (15% of users)

**Demographics**:
- Age: 30-50
- Status: Currently working at sea
- Internet: Limited (satellite internet, port wifi)
- Income: $6,000-$15,000/month
- Location: Worldwide (ships)

**Psychographics**:
- Goals: Plan next contract, track documents
- Pain Points: Limited internet, document management
- Behavior: Uses apps in port only
- Tech: Moderate proficiency, needs simple UI

**Message**: "Manage your career from anywhere, even at sea"

## Go-to-Market Plan

### Phase 1: Soft Launch (Month 1-2)

**Goal**: 1,000 beta users, validate product-market fit

**Channels**:
1. **Maritime Academy Partnerships**
   - Present at BMMTA, MAMI career days
   - Distribute flyers with QR code
   - Target: 500 students

2. **Seafarer Facebook Groups**
   - Post in "Bangladeshi Seafarers" groups
   - Share screenshots, testimonials
   - Target: 300 seafarers

3. **Word of Mouth**
   - Referral program (future)
   - Ask early users to share
   - Target: 200 organic users

**Content**:
- "We're launching BD Mariner Hub - Beta access available now"
- Highlight: Free, easy to use, all jobs in one place
- Call to action: "Join beta at bdmarinerhub.com"

---

### Phase 2: Public Launch (Month 3-4)

**Goal**: 5,000 monthly active users (MAU)

**Channels**:
1. **Press Release**
   - Submit to Daily Star, Prothom Alo (tech sections)
   - Focus: Innovation in maritime recruitment
   - Target: 1,000 users from PR

2. **Facebook Ads**
   - Target: Males 22-45, interested in maritime, shipping
   - Budget: $500/month
   - Target: 2,000 users

3. **YouTube Demos**
   - Create video tutorials in Bengali and English
   - SEO-optimized titles
   - Target: 500 users

4. **Manning Agent Partnerships**
   - Approach top 10 agencies
   - Co-marketing opportunity
   - Target: 1,500 users

**Content**:
- Product demo videos
- Feature highlights
- User testimonials
- How-to guides

---

### Phase 3: Growth (Month 5-12)

**Goal**: 20,000 MAU, 50% market penetration

**Channels**:
1. **SEO Content**
   - Blog posts on maritime careers
   - Job search tips
   - Certification guides
   - Target: 5,000 organic visitors/month

2. **Community Building**
   - Active forum with valuable content
   - Alumni association events
   - Virtual career fairs
   - Target: Engaged community

3. **Influencer Marketing**
   - Partner with senior mariners on YouTube
   - Sponsored content
   - Target: Credibility boost

4. **TV/Radio (Optional)**
   - Ads on maritime-focused media
   - Budget: $2,000/month
   - Target: Brand awareness

#### Job Listing Pages

**URL Structure**: `/jobs/[job-id]/[slug]`
**Example**: `/jobs/550e8400/chief-engineer-abc-maritime`

**Title Template**: "{Rank} - {Agency} | BD Mariner Hub"
**Meta Description**: "{Rank} job at {Agency}. Salary: {Salary}. Joining: {Date}. Apply now on BD Mariner Hub."

**Schema Markup** (JSON-LD):
```json
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org/",
  "@type": "JobPosting",
  "title": "Chief Engineer",
  "description": "Chief Engineer needed for Supramax Bulk Carrier",

  "employmentType": "CONTRACTOR",

  },

    "@type": "Place",

      "@type": "PostalAddress",

    "currency": "USD",
```
